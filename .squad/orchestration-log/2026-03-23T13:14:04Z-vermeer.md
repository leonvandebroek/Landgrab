## Summary
Vermeer fixed two distinct frontend issues affecting compass tracking and player location broadcast timing:

1. **Compass crash:** Perpetual `requestAnimationFrame` loop in `lerpBearing` was continuously mutating the map bearing even after convergence, driving Leaflet CSS transforms at 60fps and causing OOM crashes 30–60 seconds after enabling heading tracking. Fixed by adding convergence check (`Math.abs(diff) < 0.3`) that returns early and prevents further rAF scheduling. Added `lerpBearingRef` and `compassHeadingRef` for stable closure refs. Q/E handler now registers once with empty dependency array.

2. **Location throttle:** `UpdatePlayerLocation` hub invocations were throttled to at most once per 3000ms, causing up to 3-second delay in proximity reveal after movement. Reduced `LOCATION_BROADCAST_THROTTLE_MS` to 750ms — still coalesces rapid GPS updates but sends within imperceptible latency.
